What do UltimateFTC do and offers?

UltimateFTC is a company that creates and design high quality football trading card for all, it is easy to find and affordable.

  • Our football trading card is affordable, easy to find and most importantly high quality.
  • Our cards features players from the big six club in the premier league and also some of the icons.
  • Our own app will notify you if there's any updates on our product and the latest football news.
  • Our cards will regularly updated if there's any updates from the news and every season.

UltimateFTC is the ultimate home for football fans. We bring you more than just match scores you’ll find the fiercest rivalries, the most exciting stats, and loads of fun fan content all in one place. Whether it’s a heated derby or a big match showdown, we’ve got the passion and energy covered.
